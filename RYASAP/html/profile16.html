Hartford Program Manager


Shanee Ransom

Shaneer@publicallies.org

     "Shanee L. Ransom, MFA, is the Hartford Program Manager at Public Allies Connecticut/RYASAP, Inc. Her responsibilities will include: recruitment, selection, and management of Allies and partner nonprofit organizations; curriculum development; data management and reporting; public relations, marketing, and general liaison functions. As a key member of the management team, Shanee will share responsibility for all aspects of Ally management and for delivering high quality, high impact leadership development to emerging leaders across the state.

Shanee brings fifteen years of workforce development and program management experience and a strong track record focused on expanding access to education and employment opportunities for individuals who have been traditionally underrepresented and underserved. Prior to joining Public Allies, Shanee served as a workforce development specialist at Our Piece of the Pie, Inc. (OPP), in Hartford, Connecticut, where she leveraged her background in the arts to develop and execute creative strategies in support of a number of programmatic initiatives. While at OPP, Shanee also assisted industry partners in developing, assessing, and retaining talent – managing a portfolio of nearly 30 industry partnerships over the course of her tenure. Shanee has also served as a workplace campaign manager at the Greater Hartford Arts Council.

Shanee is a member of the National Association of Workforce Development Professionals (NAWDP) and holds a Certified Workforce Development Professional (CWDP) credential awarded by NAWDP. In addition, she holds a bachelor’s degree in theatre from Florida A&M University and a master of fine arts in stage management from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. In her free time Shanee is a prolific playwright and theatre artist and an engaged member of the Greater Hartford community."

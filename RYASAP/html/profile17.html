Austin Terreri

austin@ryasap.org

     Austin Terreri, Prevention Corps Program Director, is a native of Connecticut. He graduated from Eastern Connecticut State University with a Bachelor’s of Arts in Sociology. Upon completion of his college degree, Austin served for two years as an AmeriCorps VISTA for Montana Campus Compact. Post-service Austin moved back to Connecticut with his wife, Olivia Carney, to take a position with Connecticut Campus Compact. Since 2017, Austin has worked with his colleagues at Regional Youth Adult Social Action Partnership (RYASAP) to create and run the Prevention Corps Program. Prevention Corps is an AmeriCorps initiative that concentrates on substance use prevention and harm reduction with a specific focus on opioids. Currently, Austin lives with his wife Olivia, and dog Patsy, in Wallingford, CT.

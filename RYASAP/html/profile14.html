Emily Gallagher

Director of Compliance and Ally Development


Emily Gallagher

emilyg@publicallies.org

      "Emily Gallagher joined the Public Allies Connecticut staff as a Program Manager in 2009. In the intervening years, she has coordinated local partnerships and coached over 100 diverse Public Allies AmeriCorps members in the greater New Haven area. In addition to her work with Allies, Emily has strengthened Connecticut's signature program by developing and delivering new trainings, expanding partnership networks, and strengthening internal policies and systems to ensure compliance with grant requirements. In 2016, Emily was promoted to Director of Compliance and Ally Development and continues to manage our New Haven cohort; empowering members to achieve their goals through individualized coaching and support.

Emily is a proud Public Allies Connecticut Alum from the classes of 2008 and 2009. In 2012, Emily served as a US Department of State Fellow, exchanging best practices and consultation with emerging Turkish social entrepreneurs through the Young Turkey - Young America Program. In her free time, Emily enjoys travel, gardening, and supports a number of social justice causes. Emily holds a Bachelor of Science in Human Services from Albertus Magnus College, and a Masters in Business Administration from Southern Connecticut State University."

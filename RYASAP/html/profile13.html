Lindsey Tengatenga

Executive Director of Public Allies


Lindsey Tengatenga

lindseyt@publicallies.org

      "Lindsey Tengatenga is the Executive Director of Public Allies Connecticut (PACT), an organization working to create a more just and equitable society and the diverse leadership to sustain it. In this role, Tengatenga uses her experience in team development, adult learning, leadership coaching, creativity and collaboration to support homegrown, emerging leaders and organizations in Connecticut’s major cities to make meaningful change. She serves as a Coach to participants in the Leadership Development Roundtable (LDR) program, which advances mission-driven leaders throughout Connecticut by providing strengths-based, experiential professional development support to nonprofit leaders, accelerating their leadership development and equipping them with the mindset, knowledge and skills to lead organizations effectively. She is also a faculty member for the Public Allies Results Based Leadership Institute.

Prior to joining PACT in 2011, she received both her undergraduate and graduate degrees from Trinity College. She completed her Master's degree in Public Policy while working as the Assistant Director for the college's Office for Community Service and Civic Engagement developing programs to support student leaders in their passion projects and fostering community partnerships. A Chicago native, her volunteer work in Connecticut since she arrived in 2004 has been largely centered around community dialogue and civic participation efforts, policy work to support youth with incarcerated parents, farmers’ markets, and various local boards. Lindsey now lives in New Haven with her husband and 3-year-old daughter and loves to frequent her neighborhood library, coffee shops, and hiking trails, play volleyball, and root for the Cubs."

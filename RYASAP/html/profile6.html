Josel Duque

Administrative Support


Josel Duque

josel@ryasap.org

     A Bridgeport resident since 1986. Josel N. Duqué worked in banking for many years as a teller, customer service representative and became the first Latino Supervisor for People’s United Bank, Credit Card Division. He also worked for I. Brown Brothers (Interior Design Firm) for over 17 years as an Assistant Manager. While at I. Brown Brothers he learned sales and was influenced to gain design skills.

Josel N. Duqué also worked for Recovery Network of Programs at their corporate office for over five years as an Administrative Assistant. Josel was the coordinator and responsible point of contact for all the organization’s events and trainings. He was also assigned to many of their recovery sites for much needed re-organizing of their file departments while he also served as the front desk administrator.

Currently employed at RYASAP, those that are fortunate to visit the office can immediately see the organizational changes. One of my goals at RYASAP is to learn new things and share my office skills to help others to become more organized in all areas.

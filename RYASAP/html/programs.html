Our Programs

Juvenile Review Board

Juvenile Review Board diverts youth under 18 away from arrest and juvenile court & disciplinary proceedings for low level crimes and status offenses.


Read More

Mediation Services

Mediation integrates restorative justice practices for juvenile offenders, victims, and their advocates to address conflict, repair harm done & prevent further involvement in the juvenile justice system.


Read More

Street Safe Bridgeport

StreetSafe Bridgeport employs outreach workers from Bridgeport with neighborhood credibility to reduce gun and gang violence among youth using mentoring and partnerships for pro-social activities & employment.


Read More

Bridgeport Child Advocacy Coalition

Bridgeport Child Advocacy Coalition is committed to improving the well-being of Bridgeport’s children & families through research, education, advocacy, & mobilization.


Read More

Connecticut Juvenile Justice Alliance

CT Juvenile Justice Alliance is a team of dedicated advocates, policy makers, & community organizers committed to ending the criminalization of youth. The Alliance pursues meaningful reforms at state & local levels and facilitates dialogue amongst juvenile justice stakeholders.


Read More

Prevention Corps

Prevention Corps combats the opioid crisis by empowering AmeriCorps members to work in community health nonprofits to provide solutions through education and awareness.


Read More

Public Allies

Public Allies of CT engages 18-25 year-olds in full-time paid apprenticeships & engages them in intensive skills and leadership training through community-building projects, personalized coaching & critical reflection.


Read More

The Hub

The HUB supports and coordinates behavioral health initiatives (mental health, suicide, substance misuse, & problem gambling) in Southwest CT. The HUB raises awareness, develops priorities, & stimulates initiatives to fill gaps in service.


Read More

Mory Hernandez

Community Advocate


Mory Hernandez

mhernandez@ryasap.org

      Since 2000, Marc has been taught by amazing students, teachers, and community members in Hunts Point in the South Bronx, NY, New Haven, Stamford, and Bridgeport, CT. He has seen young people's lives changed by award winning culinary and urban, indoor gardening programs which have seen them featured in national television commercials for Disney and Scott's Miracle Gro and an acclaimed Michale Pollan documentary, "In Defense of Food". He is past co-chair of the Mental Health group of the Hunts Point Alliance for Children, a Congressional Black Caucus Award winner, once-upon-a-time marathoner and triathlete, and eternally frustrated sports fan.
